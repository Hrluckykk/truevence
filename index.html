WHAT WE VERIFY

Every check we run before someone joins the team.

Identity Verification

Aadhaar, PAN, passport and voter ID checks to confirm a candidate is exactly who they say they are.

Employment History

Direct confirmation with past employers on role, tenure, salary and reason for leaving.

Education Verification

Degree, diploma and marksheet authentication directly from the issuing institution.

Criminal Record Check

Court and police record screening across jurisdictions to flag undisclosed history.

Address Verification

Physical or digital confirmation of current and permanent residential address.

Medical Fitness Screening

Health and fitness-to-work assessments coordinated with certified diagnostic partners.

Reference Checks

Structured conversations with professional references to validate conduct and performance.

Global & Court Records

Cross-border and litigation record checks for candidates with an international footprint.

HOW IT WORKS

From request to report, in five clear steps.

01

Data Collection
