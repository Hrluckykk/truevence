Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Truevence uses essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website (e.g., session cookies, CSRF protection). We may also use analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site, but only with your consent.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for the site to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about site usage to help us improve.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., cookie consent).

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our site.
