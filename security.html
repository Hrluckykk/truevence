Security at Truevence

We take the protection of your data seriously. Here’s how we safeguard information.

🔒 Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Sensitive data at rest is also encrypted.

👥 Access Control

Candidate information is accessible only to authorised personnel on a strict need‑to‑know basis.

✅ Consent‑Driven

Every verification requires documented candidate consent before processing.

📋 Audit Logs

All activities are logged for accountability and can be reviewed upon request.

☁️ Cloud Infrastructure

Our platform runs on Cloudflare’s secure global network with DDoS protection and WAF.
