Background Verification Documents Checklist for HR

A complete list of documents required for employee background verification. Streamline your BGV process with this ready‑to‑use HR checklist covering identity, address, education, employment, criminal, and reference checks.

📅 July 2026 ⏱ 7 min read 👤 Truevence Editorial Team

Why a Standardised Document Checklist is Critical

Missing or incorrect documents are the #1 reason for BGV delays. A standardised checklist ensures every candidate submits the right paperwork upfront, reducing back‑and‑forth and accelerating turnaround time. It also helps you stay compliant with data privacy regulations by collecting only what's necessary.

  • ✓ Faster verification closure
  • ✓ Consistent screening across roles
  • ✓ Better candidate experience
  • ✓ Audit‑ready documentation

Documents Required for Each Verification Type

Identity Verification

These documents confirm the candidate’s legal identity.

Aadhaar CardMandatory for Indian nationals
PAN CardRequired for financial and employment checks
PassportIf available; otherwise Voter ID
Driving LicenseAdditional photo identity (optional)

Address Verification

Proof of current and permanent address.

Aadhaar CardAccepted as address proof
Utility BillElectricity/Water/Gas (not older than 3 months)
Rent AgreementIf residing in rented accommodation
Voter IDSecondary address proof
PassportCurrent address page

Education Verification

Documents proving academic qualifications.

Degree CertificateProvisional or final
MarksheetsAll semesters/years
Diploma / PG CertificateIf applicable
University Registration NumberOften required for online verification

Employment Verification

Documents to verify previous work history.

Relieving LetterFrom each previous employer
Experience CertificateWith dates and designation
Salary SlipsLast 3 months (or as required)
Offer / Appointment LetterOptional but helpful
HR Contact ReferenceName and official email

Criminal Record Check

For police or court verification, consent and identity documents are essential.

Candidate Consent FormLegally mandatory
Identity ProofAs listed above
Address ProofCurrent and permanent
Passport‑size PhotoFor police station verification

Reference Checks

Documents and details needed to conduct professional references.

Reference Name & DesignationPrevious manager or HR
Official Email & PhonePreferably company domain
Relationship with CandidateHow long they worked together
Written PermissionIf required by your policy

Candidate Consent & Data Privacy

Before collecting any documents, ensure you have the candidate’s explicit consent for background verification. This is not just good practice—it’s a legal requirement under Indian data privacy regulations. Always store documents securely and use them solely for verification purposes.

Pro Tip: Use a digital consent form that’s integrated with your BGV platform. Truevence provides built‑in candidate consent workflows to keep you compliant.

Digital Document Collection – Faster & Safer

With digital background verification, candidates can securely upload documents to a self‑service portal. This reduces paperwork, eliminates manual errors, and speeds up the process dramatically.
